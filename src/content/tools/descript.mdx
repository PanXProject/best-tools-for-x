## About
Descript revolutionizes video and podcast editing by treating media like text documents. Edit content by simply editing the transcript, with automatic synchronization between audio, video, and text. Perfect for content creators who want intuitive editing workflows.

## Features
- Edit videos by editing transcripts
- Automatic transcription with high accuracy
- AI-powered features like eye contact correction
- Remove filler words with one click
- Overdub voice cloning technology
- Multi-track editing capabilities
- Screen recording functionality
- Remote recording for interviews
- Real-time collaboration tools
- Export in multiple formats including 4K
