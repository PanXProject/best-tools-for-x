## About
Seedance 2.0 is an AI video generator for creators and teams who want fast, cinematic video generation from text prompts or images. It delivers 1080p output, advanced motion synthesis, and multi-shot storytelling in a simple web app.

## Features
- Text-to-video generation with cinematic 1080p output
- Image-to-video workflows with motion synthesis
- Multiple video models for different creative needs
