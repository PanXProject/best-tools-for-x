## About

Effect is an online visual effects generator that allows users to apply various effects to images and videos directly in their browser. The tool offers a wide range of visual effects including glitch, blur, distort, halftone, and many more creative filters for enhancing digital content.

## Features

- Apply visual effects to images and videos online
- Glitch effects and distortion filters
- Blur and halftone effects
- No software installation required
- Browser-based editing
- Free to use
- Instant preview of effects
- Export processed media
- User-friendly interface
- Wide range of creative filters
