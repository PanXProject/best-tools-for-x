## About

Lumo is Proton's private AI assistant that keeps your conversations confidential with zero-access encryption. Unlike other AI services, Lumo doesn't log, track, or share your data, giving you full control over your privacy while providing intelligent assistance.

## Features

- Zero-access encryption for all conversations
- No logs or data tracking
- Cross-platform availability (web, mobile, desktop)
- Advanced reasoning capabilities
- Multi-language support
