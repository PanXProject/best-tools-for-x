## About
Invoice Ninja lets you bill clients and accept payments, self-hosted or cloud-based. Use it to manage freelance or small biz finances. Great for keeping costs low with open-source flexibility.

## Features
- Send custom invoices
- Accept payments via Stripe
- Self-host for free
- Track time on projects
- Export financial reports
