## About
Mailmeteor is an email marketing platform, designed for sending personalized mass emails directly from Gmail or Outlook. It offers real-time tracking, advanced personalization, and seamless integration with Google Workspace.

## Features
- Schedule send for timed email delivery
- Autopilot for automated email campaigns
- BounceShield to improve deliverability
- Follow-up for automated email sequences
- Real-time email tracking for performance insights
- HTML templates for professional email design
