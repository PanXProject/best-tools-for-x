## About
Chatwoot connects with customers via live chat, email, or social, self-hosted or cloud. Use it to manage support from one dashboard without pricey subscriptions. Ideal for startups scaling customer service.

## Features
- Add live chat to your site
- Self-host for free
- Respond from a shared inbox
- Automate replies with bots
- Integrate with WhatsApp
