## About
Patternaly is a **free AI pattern generator** focused on seamless, tileable designs. Describe what you want in plain language, choose a style, and generate 1–4 variations in one click. Every output is built to repeat cleanly for real production use.

## Features
- **Text-to-Pattern** - Generate seamless patterns from prompts
- **Seamless Tiling** - Outputs are designed to tile cleanly
- **Multiple Art Styles** - Watercolor, oil painting, cartoon, 3D, kawaii, and more
- **Batch Generation** - Create 1–4 variations at once
- **High-Res Export** - Paid plans unlock high‑resolution PNGs
- **Commercial License** - Paid plans include commercial rights
