## About

BananaAI is an online AI image generation and editing platform powered by the Nano Banana model. Generate stunning visuals from text, edit photos with natural-language prompts, and turn still images into short video clips—all directly in your browser with no installation required.

## Features

- **Text-to-Image Generation** — Create high-quality images from natural language prompts in seconds.
- **Flexible Image Editing** — Convert photos into different art styles like watercolor or futuristic using simple text commands.
- **Image-to-Video** — Turn any photo into a short video clip in seconds with the built-in pipeline.
- **Multiple art styles** — Apply a wide range of looks instantly.
- **Color adjustments** — Fine-tune tones and saturation.
- **Background modifications** — Replace or edit backgrounds with ease.
- **Lighting control** — Adjust lighting and atmosphere naturally.
