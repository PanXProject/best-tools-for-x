## About
Studiovity is an AI-powered movie budgeting software that helps filmmakers create detailed production budgets, track costs, manage resources, and plan projects more efficiently. Build professional film budgets while connecting financial planning to scheduling, script breakdowns, and production workflows.

## Features
- Film Budget Creation & Cost Tracking
- Above-the-Line & Below-the-Line Budgeting
- Custom Budget Categories & Accounts
- Production Cost Estimation
- Integrated Scheduling & Production Planning
