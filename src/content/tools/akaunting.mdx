## About
Akaunting provides a free and open-source accounting solution for small businesses and freelancers. It helps manage finances, track expenses, and handle invoicing efficiently, offering a cost-effective alternative to traditional accounting software.

## Features
- Invoice and bill management
- Expense tracking and reporting
- Multi-currency support
- Client and vendor management
- Customizable dashboard
