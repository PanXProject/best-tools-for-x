## About

- Human-reviewed finishing for business-specific logic and edge cases
- Fixed-price quotes — no per-token or hourly billing
- 4–6 Week delivery, guaranteed
- 10% Upfront, 90% due only on approval
- Full code and data ownership, no platform lock-in
- Free starter templates (school management, HRMS, CRM, solar CRM)
- 200+ Apps delivered across industries
- Vertical-specific builds: construction, schools, real estate, recruitment, manufacturing, solar
