## About
Studiovity is an AI-powered shot list software that helps filmmakers, directors, and cinematographers create detailed shot lists from scripts. Plan camera angles, shot sizes, lenses, camera movements, equipment, and scene coverage while collaborating with your production team. Streamline pre-production and keep every shot organized from script to screen.

## Features
- AI Shot List Generator
- Camera Angle & Shot Type Planning
- Lens & Equipment Management
- Scene-Based Shot Organization
- Storyboard Integration
