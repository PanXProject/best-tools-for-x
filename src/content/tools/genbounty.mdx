# Genbounty - The AI Bug Bounty Platform

## About

Genbounty is the **AI bug bounty platform**. Founded in London by Cindy Ogidi and Robert Morel, Genbounty enables companies to test LLMs, agents, RAG systems, MCP servers, models, and prompts for real-world vulnerabilities - prompt injection, jailbreaks, unsafe tool use, data leakage, and more.

## Core Mission

- *"Make AI products safer by paying researchers to break them first."**

## Features
- Companies set budget, hire teams, triage findings; Genbounty manages payouts
- Companies invite teams, not individual researchers
- Structured submission workflow with severity ratings and reproduction steps

## How It Works

1. **Companies launch a program** - Define scope, budget, rules of engagement, and severity criteria
2. **Teams get invited** - Hire one or more vetted external teams or run an internal red team
3. **Researchers hunt and submit** - Teams test within scope and submit vulnerability reports
4. **Companies triage and accept** - Accept valid findings; Genbounty pays teams from your program budget

## Program Scope Types

- **Applications** - AI-powered apps and user-facing products
- **Agents** - Autonomous agents with tool access and multi-step workflows
- **RAG** - Retrieval-augmented generation pipelines and knowledge bases
- **MCP** - Model Context Protocol servers and integrations
- **Models** - Fine-tuned and hosted model endpoints
- **Prompts** - System prompts, guardrails, and prompt engineering surfaces

## Team Types

### External Teams
- Professional AI whitehat teams vetted and admin-approved by Genbounty
- Companies invite teams to specific programs
- Teams build reputation through quality submissions and leaderboard ranking

### Internal Teams
- Company-managed red team members
- Pre-approved and controlled by the company
- Ideal for confidential or ongoing testing
