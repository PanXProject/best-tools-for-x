## About
DocHub offers a comprehensive online solution for editing PDFs, signing documents electronically, and managing all your files in one place. It's a versatile tool for indie founders to streamline document workflows and reduce reliance on paper.

## Features
- Edit and annotate PDF documents
- Create and apply e-signatures
- Fill out forms online
- Cloud storage integration
- Document sharing and collaboration
