Pterocos is an A free, live HTML/CSS/JS playground with AI chat, Monaco editor & preprocessors

## It's Open Source?

Yes, its a A Opensource free browser-based coding environment for front-end developers. Write HTML, CSS, and JS with a VS Code-grade editor (Monaco), live preview, SCSS/TypeScript/Babel support, and an AI chat assistant for debugging and suggestions. All projects save to local storage. Free forever – no account needed. source code: https://github.com/aminkheddache-dotcom/Pterocos

## About

Pterocos is a browser-based development environment for HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, designed for front-end developers who want a powerful, no-cost tool without creating an account. 

One of its standout features is an AI chat assistant that can help debug code, suggest fixes, or generate UI components. All projects and backups are saved to your browser's local storage, meaning you retain full control, but be aware that clearing your cache will erase them.

## Privacy is a major focus. 

While still in beta, Pterocos is free forever, requires no login, and is open source. You can contact the developer via Discord or email. For anyone needing a lightweight, privacy-respecting coding playground with AI assistance, Pterocos offers a compelling, transparent solution.
