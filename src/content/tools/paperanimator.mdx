## About

PaperAnimator lets creators produce short frame-by-frame or timeline-based animations without complex software. It's geared toward quick explainer clips, micro-animations for product demos and social posts.

## Features

- Frame-by-frame and timeline animation modes.
- Import/export support for common image formats and MP4.
- Onion-skin previews and simple easing controls.
- Lightweight UI for quick iterations.
- Useful for freelancers producing short social/video assets.
