## About
MonicaHQ is an open-source personal CRM designed to help you organize your social interactions. It allows you to keep track of conversations, relationships, and important details about your friends and family. This tool is ideal for individuals who want to maintain stronger personal connections and remember key information about their loved ones.

## Features
- Log interactions and activities
- Manage contacts and relationships
- Set reminders for follow-ups
- Track important dates and events
- Self-host for data privacy
