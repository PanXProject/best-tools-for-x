## About
RabbitSign lets you sign documents electronically for free, without ads or watermarks, while respecting your privacy.

## Features
- Sign PDFs without watermarks  
- Fully browser-based  
- Multi-party signature support  
- Send signature requests via email  
- Upload from device or cloud storage
