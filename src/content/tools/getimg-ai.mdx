## About
Getimg.ai lets creators generate and refine visuals using AI—supporting text-to-image, inpainting/outpainting, DreamBooth model training, and video output—all starting with a free tier.

## Features
- Text-to-image and image editing  
- Inpainting & outpainting tools  
- Custom model training via DreamBooth  
- Image-to-video conversions
