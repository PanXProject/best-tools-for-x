## About
MailerLite is an intuitive email marketing platform designed for small businesses and indie founders. It simplifies creating email campaigns, managing subscribers, and building professional websites and landing pages to grow your audience.

## Features
- Drag-and-drop email editor
- Website and landing page builder
- Marketing automation workflows
- Subscriber management and segmentation
- A/B testing for campaigns
