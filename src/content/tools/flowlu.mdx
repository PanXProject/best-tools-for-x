## About
Flowlu streamlines sales, projects, and finances for small teams. Track leads, manage tasks, and send invoices in one place. The free tier suits startups with up to 2 users. Its integrated CRM, project tools, and accounting make it unique, reducing app overload.

## Features
- Track leads and contacts
- Manage tasks and projects
- Create and send invoices
- Monitor expenses
- Collaborate with teams
- View project timelines
- Generate reports
- Access on mobile
- Customize workflows
- Secure data storage
