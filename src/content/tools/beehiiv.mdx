## About

Beehiiv is the newsletter platform built by the team that scaled Morning Brew to millions of subscribers. It gives creators and businesses everything to write, grow, and monetize a newsletter in one place: a clean editor, a hosted website, referral and recommendation growth loops, and subscriber-level analytics.

## Features

- **Growth built in**: referral program, recommendations network, boosts, and magic links instead of third-party add-ons
- **Real monetization**: paid subscriptions, an ad network with sponsors, and boosted recommendations you can turn on per post
- **Serious analytics**: subscriber-level data, acquisition sources, engagement funnels, and A/B testing
- **Generous free tier**: up to 2,500 subscribers with the core editor, website, and analytics included

## Who it's for

Founders, indie makers, media brands, and any business that wants email to be a channel it owns. If your newsletter is a product rather than an afterthought, this is the platform built around that idea.
