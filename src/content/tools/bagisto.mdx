## About
Bagisto is a free and open-source e-commerce platform built on Laravel. It provides a robust framework for businesses to create and manage their online stores. This tool is suitable for developers and businesses looking for a customizable and scalable e-commerce solution with full control over their platform.

## Features
- Multi-warehouse management
- Multi-currency and multi-locale support
- Product and catalog management
- Order and inventory management
- Customizable themes and extensions
