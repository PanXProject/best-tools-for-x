## About
Temp Email provides a fast and free way to get a temporary email address. It's a lifesaver for signing up for services you don't fully trust, testing an app, or getting a quick download link without cluttering your main inbox. The emails are automatically deleted after a short period, keeping your real one safe and clean from spam.

## Features
- Create temporary email addresses instantly
- No registration or personal info required
- Automatically deletes messages for privacy
- Shields your personal email from spam
- Browser extension for quick access
