## About
Crisp unifies customer messages from chat, email, and social in one inbox. Use it to respond faster with a shared team view. Great for small businesses managing support without multiple tools.

## Features
- Centralize messages in one inbox
- Add live chat to your site
- Automate replies with bots
- See visitor details in real time
- Share inbox with team
