## About
Sora2 AI is a next-gen video generation tool that combines cutting-edge physics-based motion, synchronized audio, and cinematic quality to create stunning, realistic videos from simple text or image prompts. Whether you're a creator, marketer, or developer, Sora2 AI brings your ideas to life with unprecedented control and ease.

## Features
- Physics-Based Motion
Simulate realistic contact, inertia, and collision to make objects move and interact as they would in the real world.

- Time Consistency & Smooth Transitions
Ensure stable identity, minimal flickering, and seamless frame-to-frame transitions for a professional, polished result.

- Synced Audio
Automatically synchronize lip movements, ambient sound, and music to your video’s action for a natural, immersive experience.
