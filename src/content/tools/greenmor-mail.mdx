## About
Greenmor Mail is a custom email solution for businesses that need reliable and secure inboxes. You connect your own domain and get privacy-first policies with end-to-end encryption. A mobile-friendly webmail interface lets you send, receive, and organize mail on any device. Built-in calendar support and fast SMTP delivery powered by Amazon SES handle your day-to-day workflow. Smart spam filters keep unwanted messages out, and multiple aliases help you manage roles and signups from one inbox.

## Features
- Protected with strong encryption and strict policies keep your messages private.
- Powered by Amazon SES SMTP for fast and reliable email delivery.
- Mobile-friendly webmail interface works smoothly on any smartphone browser.
- Create multiple email aliases managed conveniently within a single inbox.
- Smart filters block unwanted messages to keep your inbox clean.
- ~ $1 / month per mailbox
