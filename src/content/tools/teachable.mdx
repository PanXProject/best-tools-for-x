## About
Teachable lets you build and sell courses online with no tech skills. Use it to share expertise or train clients. Great for educators or businesses monetizing knowledge.

## Features
- Upload videos and quizzes
- Accept payments directly
- Track student progress
- Customize course pages
- Offer certificates
