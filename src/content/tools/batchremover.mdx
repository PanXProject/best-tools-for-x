## About
BatchRemover — Bulk AI Background Remover

Remove image backgrounds instantly, one at a time for free, or upload entire folders and process up to 60 images at once with a paid plan. No login required to get started.

## What it does

[BatchRemover](https://www.batchremover.com) uses AI to detect and remove backgrounds from photos with precision — products, people, food, animals, graphics, and more. After removal, replace the background with any solid color, upload a custom image, or resize to match any platform spec. Download results one by one or as a ZIP file.

## Features

- **Free to use** — no account, no credit card, no watermarks
- **Batch processing** — upload and process up to 60 images simultaneously
- **Folder upload** — drag in an entire folder, skip the one-by-one selecting
- **Background editor** — solid color fill, custom image background, resize to spec
- **Full resolution output** — no compression, no quality loss
- **Fast AI processing** — results ready in seconds

E-commerce sellers prepping product shots, photographers cleaning up portraits, designers cutting out assets, or anyone tired of removing backgrounds one image at a time.
