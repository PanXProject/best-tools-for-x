## About
Bulk Background Remove (bulkbgremove.com) is an free AI-powered tool specifically engineered for batch image editing. Perfect for e-commerce, photography, and design professionals, it uses intelligent edge detection to flawlessly separate subjects from complex backgrounds. As a 100% web-based solution, you can upload JPG, PNG, or WebP files and download high-quality, transparent PNGs individually or as a bulk ZIP file. Fast, secure, and easy to use without any prior photo editing skills.

## Features
* **True Batch Processing:** Drag and drop dozens of images to process them simultaneously. Download your results individually or packed neatly into a single ZIP file.
* **Universal Format Support:** Easily upload JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WebP files. All processed outputs are delivered as high-quality, transparent PNG files.
* **Zero Learning Curve:** A completely web-based, user-friendly interface that requires zero technical knowledge or design skills.
* **Strict Privacy & Security:** Your data is safe. User-uploaded images are processed securely in the cloud, never shared with third parties, and strictly **not** used for AI model training.
* **Flexible Pricing:** Start for free! We offer a generous free tier, monthly subscription plans for high-volume needs, and on-demand credits for extra processing.
