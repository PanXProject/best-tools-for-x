## About
Breezy HR simplifies hiring with a free plan for one job posting. Use it to manage candidates and collaborate with a small team without cost. Perfect for startups or freelancers needing a basic ATS.

## Features
- Post one active job listing
- Manage unlimited candidates
- Collaborate with unlimited team members
- Use basic pipeline tracking
- Integrate with email for updates
