## About
KrayinCRM is an open-source CRM solution designed to help businesses manage their customer relationships effectively. It provides tools for sales, marketing, and customer service, enabling better interaction and engagement with clients. This tool is beneficial for small to medium-sized businesses seeking an affordable and flexible CRM system.

## Features
- Lead and contact management
- Sales pipeline tracking
- Email marketing campaigns
- Activity and task management
- Customizable modules
