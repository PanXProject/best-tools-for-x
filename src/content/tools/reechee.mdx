## About
Monitor negative reviews across G2, Capterra, and major platforms to identify frustrated prospects ready for alternatives.

## Features
- Competitor review monitoring across 5+ major platforms
- Automatic detection of frustrated customers
- Company intelligence and data enrichment
- Reviewer LinkedIn identification
- AI-powered pain point analysis
- Personalized sales pitch generation
- Multi-channel outreach templates (LinkedIn, email, calls)
- Integration capabilities via webhooks
- Pattern recognition across multiple reviews
