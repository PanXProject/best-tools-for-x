## About
Kit (formerly ConvertKit) offers email marketing for free up to 300 subscribers. Use it to send newsletters or sequences to a small audience. Ideal for creators building an email list on a budget.

## Features
- Manage up to 300 subscribers
- Send unlimited emails
- Create simple automation sequences
- Build landing pages
- Track basic email stats
