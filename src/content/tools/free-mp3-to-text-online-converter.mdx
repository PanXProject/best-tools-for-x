## About
mp3totext.net is a focused MP3 to text converter designed for people who work with audio every day—students recording lectures, researchers running interviews, and solo creators repurposing podcasts.
It’s browser-based and intentionally simple: upload an MP3, click Transcribe, and get a readable transcript you can use right away. You can copy the text, download a TXT file, or export SRT for subtitles.

## Features
* MP3 to text online transcription
* Copy transcript text instantly
* Download as TXT
* Export as SRT subtitles
* Edit the transcript text after it’s generated (for copy/TXT)
* No credit card required to start
* One-time credit packs (no subscription)
* Credits never expire
