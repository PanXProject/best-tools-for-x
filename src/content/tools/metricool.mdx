## About
Metricool schedules posts and tracks performance across social platforms like Instagram and Facebook. Its free plan covers one brand with basic analytics, making it ideal for creators or small businesses growing their online presence.

## Features
- Schedule up to 50 posts per month
- Track basic metrics like reach and engagement
- Manage one brand across multiple platforms
- Plan content with a visual calendar
- Export simple performance reports
