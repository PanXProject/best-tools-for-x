## About
Prime Teams is an all-in-one platform for managing projects, tracking time, and monitoring team productivity. It helps remote, hybrid, and agency teams stay aligned, accountable, and productive using a single, easy-to-use system.

## Features
- Project and task management with Kanban boards
- Automatic time tracking
- Live screen monitoring
- Screenshot capture for work visibility
- Attendance and work hour tracking
- Team and role management
- Productivity and performance reports
- Support for remote and hybrid teams
- Centralized dashboard for managers
- Reduces reliance on multiple tools
