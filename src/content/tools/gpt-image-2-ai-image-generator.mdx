## About
GPT Image 2 is an AI image generator and editor powered by the GPT-Image-2 model. It helps creators, marketers, and sellers turn text prompts or references into polished visuals in seconds.

## Features
- Text-to-image generation for marketing, product, and social visuals
- Plain-English image editing with fast browser-based iteration
- Reference remix workflows for style matching and batch consistency
