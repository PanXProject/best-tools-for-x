## About
HubSpot CRM is a free tool designed to help startups manage leads, track deals, and build customer relationships. Its intuitive interface makes it perfect for small teams starting with sales and marketing.

## Features
- Unlimited users and contacts
- Track leads and deals in real-time
- Email integration for customer communication
- Pipeline management for sales
- Free with no setup costs
