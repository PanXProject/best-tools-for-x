## About
Supernormal automatically records and transcribes meeting notes using AI, integrating smoothly with popular video platforms like Zoom and Google Meet.

## Features
- Automatic transcription of meetings  
- AI-generated smart summaries  
- Supports Zoom, Google Meet, Teams  
- Saves notes automatically  
- Multilingual support  
- Export notes in multiple formats  
- Searchable meeting transcripts
