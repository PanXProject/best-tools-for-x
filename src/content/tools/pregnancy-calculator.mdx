## About
DueDateCalc is the fastest way to calculate the exact number of days, weeks, or months between any two dates. Whether you are tracking a project deadline, counting down to a vacation, or calculating an invoice due date, get a precise result instantly without any distracting ads or sign-up forms.

## Features
- **Instant Day Count:** Enter start and end dates for an immediate total of days, weeks, and months.
- **Add/Subtract Logic:** Find a future or past date by adding or subtracting a specific number of days from a start date.
- **Business Day Toggle:** Exclude weekends and holidays to calculate only working days (coming soon).
- **Mobile Optimized:** Works flawlessly on desktop, tablet, or phone for on-the-go planning.
- **Privacy First:** All calculations happen in your browser. No date data is ever stored or tracked.
- **Clear Visual Output:** Results are displayed in a large, readable format with a breakdown of the time span.
