## About
Squoosh lets you compress and optimize images directly in your browser without uploading them to a server.

## Features
- Lossless and lossy image compression  
- Supports multiple image formats  
- Works offline in the browser  
- Real-time quality comparison preview  
- Adjustable compression settings  
- Drag and drop support
