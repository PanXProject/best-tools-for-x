## About
Freshdesk turns customer queries into trackable tickets. Use it to manage support across email and chat for bigger teams. Great for businesses scaling up customer service operations.

## Features
- Convert emails to tickets
- Assign tickets to agents
- Automate repetitive responses
- Track resolution times
- Integrate with Slack
