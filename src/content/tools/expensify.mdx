## About
Expensify tracks receipts and expenses for teams on the go. Use it to scan receipts and submit reports fast. Perfect for businesses managing travel or project costs.

## Features
- Scan receipts with camera
- Submit expenses via app
- Approve reports in bulk
- Sync with accounting tools
- Track mileage automatically
