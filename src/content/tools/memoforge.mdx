## About

MemeForge is a free, browser-based meme generator that runs entirely on the client side. No sign-ups, no watermarks, no ads. Drag & drop images, add text with full control over font and color, apply 12+ filters, and download high-quality PNGs. All processing happens locally – your images never leave your device.

## Features

- Privacy-first – all processing happens locally in your browser
- Drag & drop or paste images (Ctrl+V) for instant upload
- Add customizable top/bottom text with full control over font, color, and outline
- 12+ Built-in filters including grayscale, sepia, vintage, noir, and psychedelic
- Live preview as you edit – see changes instantly
- Export high-quality PNG images or copy directly to clipboard
