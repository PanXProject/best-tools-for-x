## About
Bitwarden stores passwords securely across devices with open-source code. Self-host it for team access control. Use it to manage logins without relying on closed-source alternatives.

## Features
- Sync passwords across devices
- Self-host for private vault
- Generate strong passwords
- Share logins with team
- Audit security with reports
