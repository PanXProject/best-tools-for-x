## About
AI Dog Dancing turns a single dog photo into a short dancing video you can share anywhere. Instead of learning video editors or complex motion tools, you upload one image, choose a dance template, and generate a preview in minutes. The result is an MP4 optimized for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts, so you can post instantly without extra editing.

## Features
- Photo‑to‑video generation
- Dance templates (meme, celebration, cute, trend)
- MP4 download for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- Fast preview, mobile‑friendly workflow
- Generate variations for cleaner results
