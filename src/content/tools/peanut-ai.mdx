## About
Peanut AI is an all-in-one AI video creation tool under Bilibili, featuring core capabilities of text-to-video and audio-to-video. It can automatically split scripts, match copyrighted materials, generate intelligent subtitles and multi-style AI dubbing, and support MG animation production. Without professional editing skills, ordinary users can quickly produce 1080P high-definition videos for self-media updates, popular science, product promotion and podcast visualization.
