## About

TypeRacer is a competitive typing game where players race against others by typing passages from books, movies, and songs. Track your words per minute and compete in real-time multiplayer races to improve typing speed and accuracy.

## Features

- Real-time multiplayer racing
- Text from books, movies, and songs
- WPM and accuracy tracking
- Progress statistics and history
- Custom race creation
- Friend challenges
- Typing certificates
- Global leaderboards
