## About

Uncover search insights and content ideas by visualizing what people ask about your topics. Essential for content creators, marketers, and SEO professionals.

## Features

- Visual search data representation
- Question-based keyword research
- Preposition and comparison insights
- Search volume and trend data
- Content gap identification
- Multiple language support
- Export capabilities for data analysis
- Search autocomplete insights
- Competitor content research
- Content planning workflows
