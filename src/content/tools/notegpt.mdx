## About
NoteGPT is an AI assistant designed to boost your learning and productivity. It can quickly generate summaries and key takeaways from a variety of sources like YouTube videos, PDFs, and articles. Instead of spending hours reading or watching, you get the essential information in seconds. It's a great tool for students, researchers, or anyone who needs to digest a lot of information quickly.

## Features
- Summarize YouTube videos instantly
- Transcribe video content with timestamps
- Summarize articles and documents
- AI chat to ask questions about content
- Create AI-generated mind maps and flashcards
