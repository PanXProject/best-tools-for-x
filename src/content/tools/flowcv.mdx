## About
FlowCV is an intuitive online tool that helps you build a job-winning resume and cover letter. It offers modern, professional templates and guides you through the process, ensuring your document is formatted correctly and optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) used by recruiters. It's a great tool to streamline your job application process.

## Features
- Modern, professional resume templates
- ATS-ready formatting for job applications
- Easy-to-use editor with real-time preview
- Customizable layouts and designs
- Includes a cover letter builder
