## About
Trello uses Kanban boards to help small teams organize tasks and projects. Its free tier is perfect for bootstrappers coordinating workflows, with mobile and web access for flexibility.

## Features
- Unlimited cards and up to 10 boards
- Drag-and-drop task management
- Mobile app for on-the-go updates
- Integrates with other tools
- Freemium model with premium upgrades
