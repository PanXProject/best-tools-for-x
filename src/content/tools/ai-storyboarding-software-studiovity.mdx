## About
Studiovity is an AI-powered storyboarding software that helps filmmakers convert scripts into visual storyboards, organize scenes, plan shots, and collaborate with production teams. Streamline pre-production with AI-generated storyboard frames and integrated filmmaking workflows.

## Features
- AI Storyboard Generation
- Script-to-Storyboard Conversion
- Scene & Shot Visualization
- Camera Angle & Shot Planning
- Storyboard Collaboration & Sharing
