## About
EngageBay combines CRM, email marketing, and sales automation. Manage leads and send emails with a free tier for 250 contacts. Perfect for startups, it offers an all-in-one solution, reducing the need for multiple tools compared to competitors.

- Send email campaigns
- Manage leads
- Track sales pipeline
- Automate marketing
- Offer live chat
- Build landing pages
- Manage social media
- View analytics
- Integrate apps
- Access on mobile
