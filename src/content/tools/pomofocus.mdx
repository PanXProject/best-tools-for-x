## About
PomofocuS is a clean, distraction-free pomodoro timer that helps you maintain focus during work sessions. It follows the proven 25-minute work, 5-minute break technique to boost productivity. Perfect for students, remote workers, and anyone struggling with time management and maintaining concentration.

## Features
- Classic 25/5 minute pomodoro cycles
- Customizable timer durations
- Task list integration
- Progress tracking and statistics
- Desktop notifications
- Minimalist, ad-free interface
- Keyboard shortcuts for quick control
- Dark and light themes
- Session history tracking
- White noise integration
