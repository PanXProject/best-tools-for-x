## About
Bing Image Create is a powerful AI tool that lets you turn your ideas into stunning visuals. Simply type a description of what you want to see, and the AI will generate it for you. It's perfect for creating artwork, illustrations, or even unique backgrounds without any design skills. It also includes video creation, allowing you to bring short scenes to life from a text prompt.

## Features
- Generates images from text using AI
- Creates videos from text prompts
- Fast and standard generation speeds
- Free to use with a Microsoft account
- Accessible on web and mobile app
