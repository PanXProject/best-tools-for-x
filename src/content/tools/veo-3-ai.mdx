## About
Veo 3 AI Video Generator with Realistic Sound is your all-in-one content creation platform. Whether you have a single image or a story idea, Veo 3 transforms it into a fully animated video — complete with natural motion, synchronized voice, and cinematic flair.

## Features
- Dual Generation Modes
• Text → Video: Describe your scene, character, or moment in words, and Veo 3 will bring it to life.
• Image → Video: Upload an image (JPG/PNG, up to 10 MB), and Veo 3 animates it — facial motion, body movement, environment transitions.
- Realistic Sound & Lip Sync
Audio is a first-class citizen: Veo 3 ensures speech and voiceover sync naturally with lip movement, and ambient sound effects enhance immersion.
