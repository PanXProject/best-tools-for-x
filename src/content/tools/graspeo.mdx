## About
Graspeo is a **free AI quiz generator** that instantly creates interactive quizzes from any content source. Whether you have a PDF document, text notes, or a YouTube video, Graspeo's AI analyzes the content and generates high-quality quiz questions in under 10 seconds.

## Features
- **PDF to Quiz** - Upload any PDF (textbooks, lecture slides, training manuals) and get a complete quiz instantly
- **Text to Quiz** - Paste notes, articles, or any text content to generate practice questions
- **YouTube to Quiz** - Turn any YouTube video into a comprehension quiz using video transcripts
- **AI Flashcard Maker** - Create study flashcards from your content automatically
