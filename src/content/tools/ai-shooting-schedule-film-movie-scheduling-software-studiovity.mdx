## About
Studiovity is an AI-powered film scheduling and production management platform that helps filmmakers create shooting schedules, stripboards, call sheets, production calendars, and day-out-of-days reports from script breakdowns. Plan, organize, and collaborate on film, TV, commercial, and video productions from web, iOS, and Android.

## Features
- AI-Powered Shooting Schedule Creation
- Digital Stripboard & Scene Scheduling
- Automatic Call Sheet Generation
- Day-Out-of-Days (DOOD) Reports
- Cast, Crew & Location Scheduling
