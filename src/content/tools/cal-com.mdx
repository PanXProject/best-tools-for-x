## About
Cal.com is an open-source scheduling platform designed to streamline appointment booking for businesses, teams, and professionals. It offers customizable booking links, calendar integrations, and robust automation, making it easy to manage meetings, events, and client appointments. 

## Features
- Customizable booking links and pages
- Multiple calendar integrations (Google, Outlook, etc.)
- Video conferencing integration (Zoom, Google Meet)
- Automated workflows and reminders
- Recurring and one-off event scheduling
- Team scheduling with round-robin distribution
- Payment collection for paid appointments
- Secret meeting links for premium clients
