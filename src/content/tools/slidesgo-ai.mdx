## About
Slidesgo AI creates professional presentations automatically from your topic or content. It combines AI-powered design with thousands of premium templates to produce presentation-ready slides in minutes. Perfect for business professionals, educators, and students who need polished presentations quickly.

## Features
- AI-generated presentation outlines
- Thousands of professional templates
- Automatic content suggestions
- Custom branding and colors
- Multiple export formats
- Real-time collaboration
- Stock photo integration
- Icon and illustration library
- Speaker notes generation
- Mobile editing capabilities
