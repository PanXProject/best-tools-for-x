## About
Affordable, Scalable User Authentication for Global Reach

Akedly.io is a local alternative to Twilio, purpose-built to eliminate the high costs associated with user authentication, both nationally and internationally. Designed with cost efficiency and reliability in mind, Akedly leverages a proprietary smart orchestration algorithm that automatically selects the most affordable and effective communication channel—SMS, WhatsApp, or other—to ensure seamless user verification.



## Features
- ✅ We enable businesses to reach customers anywhere in the world at national pricing, without compromising on delivery speed or security.

- ✅ Whether you're verifying users in Cairo or California, Akedly helps you do it smarter, faster, and more affordably.
- ✅ Pay in local currency (EGP)
- ✅ Up to 10x cheaper than Twilio.
- ✅ Pay for successful authentications only!
- ✅ Built for developers, startups, and enterprises scaling across borders.
- ✅ No upfront Commercial Registration needed.
