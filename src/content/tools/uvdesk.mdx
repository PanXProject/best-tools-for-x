## About
UVdesk is an open-source helpdesk and ticketing system designed to enhance customer support. It allows businesses to manage customer inquiries, track support tickets, and provide efficient resolutions. This tool is ideal for companies aiming to improve their customer service operations and streamline communication with clients.

## Features
- Centralized ticket management
- Knowledge base for self-service
- Email and social media integration
- Agent collaboration tools
- Customizable workflows
