## About

CasePost is a free online text utility toolkit built for writers, 
developers, and anyone who works with words. No account required. 
No paywalls. No clutter. Just paste your text, pick your format, 
and copy the result in one click.

---

## Features

- **9 case conversion formats** — UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, 
  Sentence case, camelCase, PascalCase, snake_case, kebab-case, aLtErNaTe
- **Word Counter** — tracks words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs
- **Remove Extra Spaces** — cleans up whitespace and double spaces instantly
- **Reverse Text** — flips text backwards word by word or character by character
- **Remove Line Breaks** — strips unwanted line breaks from copied text
- **Live word and character count** — updates as you type or paste
- **One-click copy to clipboard** — no selecting, no dragging
- **Use as input** — chain conversions without repasting
- **Zero signup required** — open and use, nothing else
- **100% free** — no tiers, no limits, no catches
