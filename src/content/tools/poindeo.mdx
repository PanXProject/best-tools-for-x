## About
Poindeo specializes in creating engaging product demonstrations and tutorials with one-click zoom effects. Perfect for showcasing features and creating social media content without complex video editing skills.

## Features
- One-click zoom animations on images and videos
- Beautiful background templates
- Dynamic music integration
- Preset social media aspect ratios
- 4K video output capability
- GIF animation creation
- Screen recording functionality
- Branding and watermark options
