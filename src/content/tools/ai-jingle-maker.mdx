## About
AI Jingle Maker is an online tool that creates professional radio jingles, podcast intros, and audio ads from text. Users write a short script, choose from 16 AI voices, select background music, and download a complete jingle in 10-30 seconds. No music production skills required. All generated content comes with commercial rights for use on radio, YouTube, TikTok, podcasts, and any advertising platform. New users get 10 free credits to try.

## Features
- Text to Jingle 
- 16 AI Voices 
- Music Library
- One-Click Compose
