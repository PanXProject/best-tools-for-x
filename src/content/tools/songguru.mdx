## About
SongGuru lets you generate complete songs from a simple prompt or your own lyrics. It composes music, writes lyrics, and performs vocals across genres, delivering high-quality tracks in seconds. Choose styles and instruments, customize mood and structure, or create instrumentals. Download MP3s for personal or commercial use and share instantly. Start free with daily credits and upgrade for higher limits.

## Features
- AI Song Generator
Generates free-to-use, high-quality songs in seconds.

- AI Lyrics Generator
Generate unique, compelling lyrics instantly.

- AI Vocal Remover
Isolate the instrumental track or the vocal track from any song.
